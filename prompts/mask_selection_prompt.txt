Given an image panel, a referring expression, and candidate masks, choose the mask
that best matches the target.

Return strict JSON only:
{
  "best_index": 0,
  "reason": "brief reason"
}

Rules:
- Candidate indexes are drawn in the panel from left to right.
- Prefer the mask that covers the complete referred target and excludes distractors.
- If the target is a part or region, prefer the part/region rather than the whole object.
- Do not include markdown or explanations outside JSON.
